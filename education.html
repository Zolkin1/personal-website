Master Peer Instructor at The Hive Makerspace

I have been a Peer Instructor at The Hive Makerspace since August 2019, and in May 2020 I became a Master Peer Instructor (MPI) for the benchtop electronics area. As a MPI I teach students who come into the makerspace how to use the tools such as soldering equipment, function generators and oscilloscopes. I also provide advice when asked and give parts to students for their projects.

Years: 2019-Present

FIRST Lego League (FLL) Mentoring Experience

I worked with Casa De La Esperanza and Up-A-Creek Robotics to mentor ~4 FLL teams each year. I would help the students understand basic engineering concepts, iterate on their ideas, and learn to present their ideas to judges. I loved to help the students see their ideas come to fruition and see them be able to explain their ideas to others.

Number of Teams Mentored: 12
Years: 2014-2017

VEX IQ Mentoring Experience

I worked with the St. Vrain Valley School district in Longmont, CO to develop an after school robotics curriculum. The final version of the curriculum contained 12 weeks of activities for elementary and middle school teachers who had no prior robotics experience. This allowed the teachers to effectively run robotics teams in the VEX IQ program.

Number of Teams Mentored: ~20
Years: 2014-2018
