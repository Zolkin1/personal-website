About Me

Hi!

I am un undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Gerogia Tech. I am a maker who loves to work with robots, electronics, and embedded systems. I began working with Lego robots in 4th grade and since then I have had the opportunity to work on larger, competitive, robots through competitions such as Robocup and First Robotics Competition. I also have experience with robots used in industry from working at Left Hand Robotics. Currently, I do research on quadrotor controls and trajectory planning at Georgia Tech in the iREAL Lab. My main intrests are controls and dynamics, navigation, and state estimation. I see great value in implementing these systems in real-time so they can be used in the real world so I always look to implement my ideas on hardware. I want to make robots that improve the world, so I always make sure my work has real world applications and feasability.
